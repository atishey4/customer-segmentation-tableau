## Overview
This Tableau project segments customers by demographics and purchase behavior to identify high-value groups and reveal buying patterns that guide targeted marketing and retention strategies.

## Sample Dataset (key fields)
- Customer ID, Customer Name, Age, Gender
- City/Region, Occupation, Income Level
- Customer Segment, Product Category, Product Name
- Purchase Frequency, Average Order Value, Total Spending
- Payment Method, Purchase Channel (Online/Offline/App)
- Last Purchase Date, Customer Loyalty Status
- Discount Usage, Customer Satisfaction Score

## KPIs & Calculations
- Total Customers, Total Revenue, Average Order Value
- Purchase Frequency, Repeat Customers %
- Customer Lifetime Value (basic)
- Segment-wise Revenue, Loyalty Score
- Discount Impact Analysis, Most Popular Product Category

## Dashboard Views
- KPI cards for Customers, Revenue, AOV
- Revenue by Customer Segment (bar)
- Product Category Performance (column)
- Purchase Trend over time (line)
- Customer Segment Distribution (donut)
- Region vs Spending (heatmap)
- Income vs Spending (scatter)
- Customer analysis table with filters (Region, Segment, Category, Channel)

## Insights Enabled
Identify the most valuable segments, preferred products, regional spending hotspots, and repeat-purchase behavior to optimize campaigns and offers.
